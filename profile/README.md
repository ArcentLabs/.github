# ArcentLabs

### AI for in-silico prioritization in healthy ageing research

ArcentLabs develops specialized computational systems for translational ageing biology.

Our work focuses on helping researchers prioritize healthy-ageing intervention candidates before costly and time-consuming laboratory validation.

## GEROTWIN

GEROTWIN is an AI-based virtual aged-tissue model designed to predict and rank the expected response of physiologically aged tissue to geroprotective interventions.

The platform supports earlier, evidence-informed decision-making through:

- Counterfactual intervention simulation
- Physiologically aged single-cell atlases
- Uncertainty-aware candidate ranking
- Explainable computational predictions
- Reproducible research methodologies

## Our mission

We build computational tools that help research teams reduce uncertainty, prioritize promising interventions and optimize physical validation pathways.

## Partnerships

We collaborate with researchers, validation laboratories and biotechnology partners working in healthy-ageing and translational biology.
